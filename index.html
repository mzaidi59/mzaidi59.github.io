About Me

I am a Research Engineer at the Next AI Solutions Lab, Samsung Research Headquarters, Seoul. At Samsung, I have been working in the field of Natural Language Processing and Speech Recognition.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree with a major in Electrical Engineering and minors in Computer Science(Machine Learning) and Cognitive Sciences from IIT Kanpur. Upon graduation I received the SPVM Gold Medal for the best academic performance among all the graduating 4-year students. I worked on developing improved wireless transmission decoder for STTC at the Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO)- IITk Space cell with Prof. Aditya Jagannatham. I also worked with Prof. Ketan Rajawat on removing the sample bottleneck constraint from Online Gaussian Processes.

I am interested in Machine Learning, Wireless Communication and Cognitive Sciences
