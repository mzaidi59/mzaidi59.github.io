About Me

As a researcher at Samsung, I have worked on a wide range of NLP tasks which include:
Automatic Speech Recognition, Machine Translation (Text and Speech), Text to Speech, Question Answering, and Neural Information Retrieval.
Recently, my research work has been focussed towards the simultaneous speech translation task.
If you are interested to know more about my work, you can have a look at my recent publications.
Feel free to contact me for any queries/discussions.
