About Me

I am a Research Engineer at the Natural Language Processing(NLP) Lab, Samsung Research, Seoul. I have been working in the Machine Translation group. Specifically, I have been working on the Simultaneous Speech Translation task.

Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree with a major in Electrical Engineering and minors in Computer Science(Machine Learning) and Cognitive Sciences from Kanpur. I was fortunate enough to work at the Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO)- IITk Space cell with Prof. Aditya Jagannatham developing improved wireless transmission decoder for Space time trellis codes. I also worked with Prof. Ketan Rajawat on removing the sample bottleneck constraint from Online Gaussian Processes.
