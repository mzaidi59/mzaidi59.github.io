Internships

Samsung Research, Seoul, South Korea

I worked as a Research Intern at the Question Answering Team in Language Understanding Lab. We built a system to generate verbose non-span based answers using words from out of vocabulary. We participated in Microsoft's Ms Marco Intermediate challenge and reached the top of the leaderboard during the internship. Results published on Samsung Newsroom website.

ISRO-IITk Space Cell, Kanpur, India

I worked on to incorporate the bounded variation property of images/videos into the Space Time Trellis Codes. We designed a hierarchichal decoder to jointly optjmize on both maximum likelihood and total variation metric. The proposed joint algorithm improved the quality of reconstructed media. Work was published in IEEE Access Journal (Volume: 8), April 2020

Pocket FM, Bangalore, India

I worked for a startup which is a Hindi(Indian language) podcast app. My work included various projects like search optmization, recommendation system, efficient/automatic error logging and reporting.

  • Machine Learning
