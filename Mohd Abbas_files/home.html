Biography

The present is the future. Our every action changes the course of the world ever so slightly. My recently discovered aim is to find a way to make it better; and the only way I can, is through research.

I am a pre-final year undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur with a major in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Artificial Intelligence. I have worked on problems ranging from Sentiment Analysis to developing new algorithms in Wireless Sensor Networks and am curretly interested in Wireless Communication, more specifically error control coding.

My broad areas of interests are: Digital Signal Processing, Neural Engineering, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Wireless Communication, and Computer Vision. Recently, I worked with Prof. Aditya Jagannatham in a project under Indian Space Reserach Organisation(ISRO) at the ISRO-IITk Space Technology Cell on developing a joint algorithm based on STTC and Total-Variation Regularisation. I modified the basic structure of how STTC and TV based regularisation is employed in an isolated scenario and merged the algorithms.

Apart from my academic work, I enjoy exploring various realms of Sociology, and it's application in our society. I enjoy documentaries related to Sociology of War/ Conflict, a big fan of John Pilger.
